About-Us

IEEE-SFIT student branch endeavours to foster technical learning and instil interpersonal competence for the overall personality development. To render a platform to explore divergent scope of learning, to kindle interest for new technologies, to actuate innovative thinking and to be the future technical pioneers of the society The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is a professional association headquartered in New York City that is dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence.The IEEE-SFIT student branch functions @SFIT and the purpose of the organisation is to achieve overall development of students
